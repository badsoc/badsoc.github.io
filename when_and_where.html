Session times

Currently our sessions are Saturdays 3pm-4pm and Sundays 3pm-4pm, during term time.
However, we recommend you check our facebook page for the session times incase this website is delayed in regards to the session times being updated.

Where to find us

Our weekly sessions take place in the Lancaster University sports centre.
Simply tell the desk staff that you are 'here for the badminton society', and they should let you through to the courts. Depending on the available booking slots, we could be in hall 1 (the old hall, and the usual location) or hall 2 (the new hall, and the backup location). But don't worry, just look around for a large group of people, (starting in hall 1) and that should be BadSoc. If you are still stuck, don't hesitate to send us a message, or look in our group chat for help.
